## 2. Major League Pickleball (MLP)

### League Structure

MLP is the preeminent, coed, team-based professional pickleball league featuring over 100 of the best athletes across teams, iconic celebrity owners, and electric live events.

**Two-Tier System:**
- **Premier Level**: 18 teams (top 108 players)
- **Challenger Level**: 5 teams (next crop of 20 players)

**2026 Season:**
- 13 events across the United States
- 9 regular season events
- Mid-Season Tournament
- Two rounds of playoffs
- MLP Finals (New York City)
- MLP Cup (November)

### Team Format: MLPlay

MLPlay is MLP's innovative gameplay format that brings men and women together on a level playing court.

**Match Structure:**
- 4 games per team match
- Games 1-2: Gender doubles (women's OR men's)
- Games 3-4: Mixed doubles
- Tiebreaker: DreamBreaker (singles format) if tied 2-2

### Scoring System

**2026 Updates - Side-Out Scoring:**
- Games played to 11 points
- Win by 2 points minimum
- Only serving team can score in doubles

**DreamBreaker (Rally Scoring):**
- Played to 21 points
- Rally scoring (point on every rally)
- Players switch out every 4 points
- "Freeze" at game point - must win on serve

**The Freeze Explained:**
When one team reaches 20 points, their score is "frozen." They can only score going forward on their own serve. The game must be won on serve.

### Draft System

**2026 Draft:**
- Teams draft full roster of 6 players
- Only UPA-signed players eligible
- Draft slots determined by bidding
- Teams can fill all six roster spots at their pace

**Roster Rules:**
- 6 players per team
- All players can be used flexibly (no starter/bench designation)
- One waiver wire period (July 1, 2026)
- Teams can swap one player during waiver period

### Standings Points

| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Regulation Win | 3 points |
| DreamBreaker Win | 2 points |

### Playoffs Structure (2026)

- 12 of 18 Premier Level teams qualify
- Round 1: Dallas
- Semifinals: San Diego
- Finals: New York City

### Group Play Format (2026)

- Each regular season event: 10 teams
- Split into 2 groups of 5
- Round robin Thursday-Saturday
- Top 4 from each group advance to Sunday

### Team Ownership

MLP boasts 80+ celebrity and professional athlete owners including:

**Sports Stars:**
- LeBron James, Tom Brady, Kevin Durant
- Patrick Mahomes, Josh Allen, Jayson Tatum
- Devin Booker, Zach LaVine, Jewell Loyd

**Entertainment:**
- Drake, Michael B. Jordan, Eva Longoria
- Heidi Klum, Kate Upton, Dude Perfect
- Gary Vaynerchuk

**Sports Legends:**
- Chris Evert, Mark Cuban, Nick Kyrgios
- Magnus Carlsen (World Chess Champion)

**Team Valuations:**
- 2021: ~$100,000
- 2025: LA Mad Drops - $13M (majority stake)
- 2026: Palm Beach Royals - $16M (expansion record)

## 3. PPA Tour (Professional Pickleball Association)

### Tournament Structure

The PPA Tour is the premier individual professional tour with a season running June-May.

**2025-2026 Season:**
- 21 total events
- 5 Cups: San Clemente, Virginia Beach, Las Vegas, Mesa, St. Louis
- 2 Slams: PPA Masters (Palm Springs), Veolia Atlanta Championships
- PPA Finals: Life Time Rancho San Clemente (May 4-10, 2026)

### Tournament Categories & Points

| Category | Points (1st Place) |
|----------|-------------------|
| Slam | 2,000 points |
| Cup | 1,500 points |
| Open | 1,000 points |
| Challenger | 125 points |

### Draw Formats

**Progressive Draws (Slams & Cups):**
- Each day devoted to a round of play
- Example: Wednesday = Round of 32

**Traditional Draws (Opens):**
- Each day devoted to a particular division

### Weekly Schedule (Typical Event)

| Day | Competition |
|-----|-------------|
| Tuesday | Qualifiers |
| Wednesday | Men's & Women's Singles |
| Thursday | Mixed Doubles |
| Friday | Men's & Women's Doubles |
| Saturday | Semifinals |
| Sunday | Finals |

### Player Rankings

**Two Ranking Formats:**
- **52 Week**: Accumulation over past 52 weeks
- **The Race**: Points earned since calendar year start

**Top Players (2025):**
- **Men's #1**: Ben Johns (7.218 DUPR singles)
- **Women's #1**: Anna Leigh Waters (6.561 DUPR singles)
- Anna Leigh Waters: Only player to triple crown in 2025 (8 times)

### Prize Money Structure

**Distribution Formula:**
- 52.5% of entry fees = Prize pool
- 1st Place: 55%
- 2nd Place: 30%
- 3rd Place: 15%

**2026+ Prize Money Model (New):**
- Performance-based compensation
- Slam 1st Place Men's Doubles: $90,000
- Round of 16: $6,000

**Player Contract Tiers:**
- Gold Contract Grid: Enhanced prize pool access
- Standard Prize Money Grid: Top 25/30 ranking required
- Futures Prize Money Grid: New players (minimum 6 months)

### Challenger Series

- 16 events in 2026 (expanded from 10)
- Players from all 50 states, 30+ countries
- Entry point for aspiring professionals
- Path to earning PPA Tour ranking points

## 7. Tournament Formats

### Single Elimination

- One loss = elimination
- Creates high-stakes atmosphere
- Best for smaller brackets
- Quick tournament completion

### Double Elimination

- Two losses = elimination
- Winners Bracket + Losers (Consolation) Bracket
- More playing opportunities
- Redemption path to finals

**Winners Bracket:**
- Best 2 of 3 games to 11, win by 2

**Consolation Bracket:**
- Single games to 15, win by 2

**Championship:**
- Winners bracket champion needs 1 win
- Losers bracket champion needs 2 wins
- Final tiebreaker to 15 if necessary

### Round Robin

- All players/teams play each other
- Best for 6 or fewer teams
- Guarantees multiple matches
- Points accumulated across all matches

### Pool Play

- Groups compete in round robin
- Top performers advance to brackets
- Combines guaranteed games with elimination excitement

### USA Pickleball Approved Formats

1. Single Elimination with Consolation
2. Single Elimination Without Consolation
3. Double Elimination
4. Round Robin
5. Pool Play
6. Non-Pool Play

## 8. Scoring Systems

### Side-Out Scoring (Traditional)

**Rules:**
- Games to 11 points
- Win by 2
- Only serving team can score
- Score announced as: Server Score - Receiver Score - Server Number
- Games start at 0-0-2 (serving team starts on second server)

**Server Rotation:**
- First server serves until rally lost
- Serve passes to second server
- Second server loses = side out
- Receiving team gains serve

### Rally Scoring

**Rules:**
- Point scored on every rally
- Games typically to 15 or 21
- Win by 2
- No second server in doubles
- 2025: USA Pickleball provisionally approved

**The Freeze (Rally Scoring):**
- At 20 points: Score frozen, must win on serve
- Trailing team at 18: Also frozen
- Prevents indefinite point trading

### Game Length Options

| Format | Points | Use Case |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Standard | 11 (win by 2) | Most common |
| Extended | 15 (win by 2) | Medal matches, consolation |
| Long | 21 (win by 2) | Rally scoring, special events |

### Benefits of Rally Scoring

- Games end ~20% faster
- More predictable duration
- Better for TV scheduling
- Faster court rotation

## 9. Division Structures

### Skill Rating Systems

**DUPR (Dynamic Universal Pickleball Rating):**
- Scale: 2.00 - 8.00
- Most accurate and widely used
- Real-time updates based on match results

| DUPR Range | Level | Description |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| 2.00-2.99 | Beginner | Basic shot consistency |
| 3.00-3.99 | Intermediate | Improving strategy |
| 4.00-4.99 | Advanced | Strong tactical awareness |
| 5.00-8.00 | Elite/Pro | Professional competition |

## 10. Team-Based Formats (3v3, 4v4)

### Minor League Pickleball (MiLP)

**Formats:**
- MiLP: Mixed 4-player teams
- MiLP v3: Gendered 3v3 teams
- MiLP v4: Gendered 4-player teams

**3v3 Format:**
- Gendered only (3 women vs 3 women OR 3 men vs 3 men)
- Three games, no DreamBreaker
- Quick match resolution
- More games per tournament day

**2025 MiLP National Championship:**
- 5 days of competition
- 50+ divisions
- Friday: MiLP v3 (3v3) Championship
- Weekend: Traditional mixed team divisions

### Swish Tournaments 3v3

**Rules:**
- Games to 15 points
- Switch sides at 8 points
- Win by 1
- Any combination: men, women, mixed

### Triple Team Pickleball League

- Based on USTA Tennis framework
- Club vs club competition
- Local, state, and national levels
- 10,000+ players in ~20 states

**Championships:**
- Richmond, VA (September)
- Dallas, TX (October)

### Global Pickleball Network Team Leagues

- Teams of 3+ players
- Team vs team with set schedule
- Different from ladder leagues
